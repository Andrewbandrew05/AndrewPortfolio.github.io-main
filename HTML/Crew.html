Crew

Sophomore Year

I joined the GT Novice Men’s Crew team at the beginning of my Sophomore semester to find community, and quickly fell in love with the sport as a way to stay in shape and compete against other schools. I spend ten to twenty hours a week training alongside my teammates and race with them in a multitude of regattas throughout the fall and spring seasons. So far I’ve collected one silver medal in the A8+ at Head of the South, and I look forward to the chance to win more next season.
