Crew

Sophomore Year

I joined the GT Novice Men’s Crew team at the beginning of my Sophomore semester to find a community, and quickly fell in love with it as a way to stay in shape and compete against other schools. I spend ten to twenty hours a week training alongside my teammates, and am currently in contention for a spot in our A8+ boat. I’m excited to see how regatta season plays out and will update this page with notable results in the coming weeks.
