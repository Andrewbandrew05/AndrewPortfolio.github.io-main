Academics

Highschool

I attended Midtown International School, a tiny private school located in Atlanta Georgia, from 7th to 12th grade. During my time there, I dual-enrolled at a variety of institutions, including Johns Hopkins Center for Talented Youth, Georgia Tech, and Oglethorpe University. I complemented the college credits I earned from my time at the later two with multiple AP tests, starting in Freshman year with AP US history and AP Biology. During this time I participated in our high school robotics team, numerous sports teams, and a band, playing a prominent role in my schools extra-curricular community. My hard work and determination paid off when I graduated as Valedictorian and became a Zell Miller Scholar.

College

I started college at Georgia Tech in the Fall of 2023. In the time since I first set foot on campus I've been inducted into Alpha Tau Beta, been trained and hired as a lifeguard at the Campus Recreational Center, and become the future head of Off-Road's Data Aquisition team. I have simultaneously helped found an on-campus band and set up a modded minecraft server on my NAS, continuing to pursue my hobbies even as my workload dramatically increased. I have yet to obtain an internship and/or co-op, but I am excited to hopefully dip my toes into the real-life workings of the tech-world sometime next summer.
