Backstory

My tiny high school had a nearly non existent tech community, and, unfortunately, I was almost alone in my extracurricular interests by Senior year. So I decided to create a tech youtube channel to connect with other techies. Over a hundred thousand views and a hundred and fifty subscribers later, I've managed to connect with people from all over the world through our shared love for electronics. (https://youtube.com/@nand1185?si=FCIzWAW3-Hu06Hev)

Water Cooled Computer

One of my more notable projects involved water cooling my old desktop computer. I purchased a multitude of used water cooling parts from Ebay, and, with a little janky engineering, successfully installed them on my computer. (https://youtu.be/T85nCTCGNuE?si=xfzqiusuRvtLJ0Uv)
